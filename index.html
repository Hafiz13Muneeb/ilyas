Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our construction services

How long does a typical construction project take?

The timeline varies based on project scope, but a typical home renovation takes 4-8 weeks, while new construction can take 4-6 months. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase and keep you updated throughout the project.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we offer free, no-obligation estimates for all projects. Our project manager will visit your site, discuss your requirements, and provide a detailed quote within 2-3 business days.

What warranties do you offer on your work?

We offer a 5-year warranty on structural work and a 2-year warranty on all other construction work. All materials used come with their manufacturer warranties, which we'll help you register.

Can you help with permits and regulations?

Absolutely. We handle all necessary permits, approvals, and ensure compliance with local building codes and regulations. Our team stays updated on all construction regulations to ensure your project meets all requirements.

How do you handle project changes or modifications?

We understand that changes may be needed during a project. We have a formal change order process where we document requested changes, provide updated pricing and timelines, and get your approval before proceeding.
